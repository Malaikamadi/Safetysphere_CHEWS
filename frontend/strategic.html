How to read this dashboard

Top KPI strip — rolling counts from every catalogued community plus district-level rainfall, soil saturation and river-stage proxies. Population at risk includes anyone in a zone scoring at least Moderate (≥0.40 composite).

Map bubbles — larger = higher numeric risk; colour maps to CHEWS severity band (legend below the map).

Scenario sliders — multiply modeled rainfall or nudge saturation, then Run scenario. The delta block compares KPIs against your first baseline load since opening the tab (hit Reset baseline by refreshing).

24h stripes — simple decay curve from the flood model (not calibrated weather-scale precision). Use it for tabletop exercises, not operational evacuation maths.

Deep link example: strategic.html#atlas/kroo_bay opens this tab and selects Kroo Bay (slug matches the catalogue id).
