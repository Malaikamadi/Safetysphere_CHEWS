Open AI · Explainable · Decision-grade

A modular ML pipeline for climate-health intelligence

CHEWS runs a multi-layer machine learning architecture: hazard-specific risk models, an epidemiological forecaster, a vulnerability engine, a healthcare readiness model, and a fusion layer that combines them into a single, explainable decision score. Every prediction ships with drivers, confidence, and a model card.

Open-Source & Digital Public Good Strategy
CHEWS is designed to register with the Digital Public Goods Alliance.
Open from day one
  • Schema + OpenAPI spec
  • Environmental, Flood, Heat models
  • Vulnerability index methodology
  • Community Reporting SDK (PWA + SMS)
Gated by data agreements
  • Country-trained weights (when trained on patient-level data)
  • Named-official escalation rules
  • NGO operations templates with org-specific data
Standards alignment
  • DHIS2 App Hub plugin (read + write)
  • DPGA 9-indicator alignment
  • Aggregated datasets on Zenodo
  • CC-BY-4.0 for aggregates
